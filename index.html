About Us

We make metal
do what you need.

Arc & Axis Mfg is a lean manufacturing shop built around one idea: get parts out the door, right the first time. We started with a laser and a welder, and we’ve been turning flat stock into finished pieces ever since.

We work with small and mid-size companies that need consistent, clean parts — whether it’s a one-off prototype bracket or a production run of 500 plates. No minimums, no attitude, no surprises.

Based in the U.S., shipping wherever you need.

Services

What we bring to the table.

🔵

Laser Cutting

Flat parts cut from steel, stainless, and aluminum. Brackets, mounting plates, gussets, flanges, panels, and custom shapes. Tight tolerances, clean edges, ready to weld or ship.

🟠

Welding & Fabrication

MIG and TIG welding for assemblies, frames, and structural components. We join what the laser cuts — finished pieces, not just flat stock.

⚪

CNC Machining

Multi-axis machining for parts that need more than a flat profile. Tapped holes, counterbores, 3D contours — coming as we expand the shop floor.
